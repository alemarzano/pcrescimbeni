Pablo Crescimbeni

During the initial stages of my career, I worked in-house, within local and international companies. At a later stage, I turned to private practice. While originally I worked within major law firms, at present I am working independently, collaborating with diverse teams depending on the nature of the projects, negotiations or conflicts faced by my clients.

I have experience with companies and clients working in varied sectors and which are at different stages in their own trajectory.

I have an extensive experience in media & telecoms and entertainment law, together with a special focus on the production and distribution of multiplatform audiovisual content, as well as creativity-based businesses (musical, artistic and technological).

I stand side by side with my clients: be it the key decision-makers of major businesses experiencing complex conflicts, or individuals who face challenges within their company, significant changes in their business environment, restructurings or business-succession issues. I aim at achieving cost-saving solutions, both from an emotional and economic viewpoint.
